What We Do

The lab bridges the gap between mathematics and real-life applications by connecting STEM education to sports. Funded by the Grand Challenges Grant, our mobile math lab travels to schools, showcasing how analytics can be used to improve sports performance.

Through hands-on activities, students interact with motion capture tools and cutting-edge analytics software, making STEM concepts practical and relatable. Coaches can collaborate with us to inspire young minds to pursue careers in STEM fields while refining athletic performance.

We focus on students traditionally underrepresented in STEM, including minorities and women, aiming to address educational disparities. By blending sports and STEM, we empower students to see the relevance of these fields in their daily lives and future careers.

Our mission: To foster an inclusive environment where learning and passion meet through innovation and outreach.
