Our Inspiration

It is no secret that kids love to play sports but may be less excited about their math homework. By bringing the Mathematical Motion Lab to schools, we show kids that mathematics can have a real impact on sports, and we harness their passion for sports to spark their interest in math and STEM.

What We Do

The lab bridges the gap between mathematics and real‑life applications by connecting STEM education to sports. Our mobile math lab travels to schools, showcasing how analytics can be used to improve sports performance.

Through hands‑on activities, students interact with motion‑capture tools and cutting‑edge analytics software, making STEM concepts practical and relatable. Coaches collaborate with us to inspire young minds to pursue careers in STEM while refining athletic performance.

We aim to address educational disparities with our work. By blending sports and STEM, we empower students to see the relevance of these fields in their daily lives and future careers.

Our mission: To foster an environment where learning and passion meet through innovation and outreach.
